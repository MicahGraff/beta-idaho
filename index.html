HERE AT THE GAMMA GAMMA CHAPTER...

We pride ourselves in excellence in academics, campus involvement, and community service for over the past 100 years. Currently, our house is the largest on campus, housing many diverse and exceptional men. On average, we have over 100 active members each year.
▼

We are located on the beautiful campus of University of Idaho, located in Moscow, Idaho. The school has a historic charm, and is located such that we get the best of the rolling hills, nearby forests, and scenic rivers. Our university has great people and numerous clubs for various interests.
