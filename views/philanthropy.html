Beta and Philanthropy

The Gamma Gamma Chapter of Beta Theta Pi strives to uphold our core value of mutual assistance through philanthropy and community service. Members are always eager to serve our community through numerous events hosted by our chapter, and actively participating in all those hosted by the University or other Greek houses. Our members accumulate an average of 20 hours per semester (some with many more), adding up to about 4,000 hours each year.

Annual Beta Philanthropies

Some annual Gamma Gamma philanthropies include: Beta Powder Puff Football, Haunted House, Woodcut, We Got Your Back 5K, Miami Triad, Greek Family Feud, Car Wash, and Highway Cleanup. New event ideas are encouraged and regularly added to our list of philanthropic events each year. Giving back to our local community is an essential value as a member of Beta Theta Pi. It teaches us invaluable lessons for us to continue helping others, even after graduation.
